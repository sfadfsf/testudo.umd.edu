GRADE POINT AVERAGE

The Grade Point Average (GPA) is based on a 4.00 scale.

GPA  =       QUALITY POINTS
---------------------------------
CREDITS ATTEMPTED

Quality points are earned from grades of A+ through D- for undergraduates and for grades of A+ through C- for graduate students. Information on how to calculate a GPA can be found in the Undergraduate Catalog.

Credits attempted include only those courses completed at the University with grades of A+ through F.

Courses numbered 799 and 899, 500 level courses and courses numbered 399 and below are not included in the GPA calculation for graduate students.

SEMESTER ACADEMIC HONORS

Semester Academic Honors are awarded to undergraduate students who complete 12 or more credits, under the regular grading option, with a semester GPA of 3.50 or better.

UNSATISFACTORY PERFORMANCE

Undergraduate students with a cumulative GPA less than 2.00 are placed on academic probation. The rules governing continued registration by probationary students can be found in the Undergraduate Catalog. Generally, students on academic probation must raise their cumulative GPA to a minimum 2.00 by the end of the subsequent semester or they will be academically dismissed.

Undergraduate students who are academically dismissed must apply for reinstatement through the Office of Student Success.
